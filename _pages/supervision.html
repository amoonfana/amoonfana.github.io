Join our team!

Our team offers 2-3 openings for Master's students each year, focused on research. Several of our students have published papers in top-tier journals, such as Information Fusion, Neural Networks, ESWA, and others. If you are interested in Pattern Recognition and Deep Learning, with a particular focus on Face Recognition, Loss Functions, Deep Neural Networks, etc. please feel free to contact me for more information.
Before applying, please ensure that you have a solid background in mathematics and programming. A strong foundation in mathematics (Calculus, Linear Algebra, Probability, and Statistics, etc.) will help you understand the methods proposed by others, while proficiency in programming (Python, Data Structures, Algorithms, etc.) is essential for implementing these methods. You can assess your level of mathematics and programming by watching my Machine Learning course. In addition to technical skills, I expect you to be self-motivated in research, as it is a long-term and challenging endeavor.
